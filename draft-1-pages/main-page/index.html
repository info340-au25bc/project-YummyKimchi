The App to Manage your Wardrobe

Keep Track. Build Oufits. Save.

FEATURES

The app offers key features needed to both manage and sort through a wardrobe. The inventory page allows you to keep track and filter through items of clothing you own. The outfit buider allows you to keep track of the status of an item of clothing (wash, basket, etc) and build clothing combinations. The account tab allows you to login or signup to remember your preferences and items.
